## Appâ€™s main goal
  
An application designed to connect all nature-lovers.
You can upload nature photos and share recommendations on points of interest and leisure in local nature.

## The App & Target Users
A scrolling shell that focuses on sharing photo content by platform users.
The app will provide an interactive platform for uploading posts with photographic content and recommendations for sites full of landscapes, animals and nature.
The user can express whether he likes, enjoys or supports an image or recommendation that another user has uploaded through the option to like and comment.
This app is for All nature lovers around the world who are interested in receiving and sharing any content related to nature and views.

## Functionality and Use-case:
<h3>1. Registration and Login page</h3>
A login page where the user will enter his user information and will be able to connect to the personal profile. If the user is not registered, the login page shows the option to register as a new user. And after entering the details the new user can connect to the profile.
